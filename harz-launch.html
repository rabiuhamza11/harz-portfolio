The Story

Founded in 2014 as HI Water/Block Industry (CAC RC 321424) and reimagined as Harz Digital Services, what started as a construction business in Bauchi State, Nigeria has become one of Africa's most ambitious technology ecosystems.

In a single year, we've architected and deployed 38 live platforms — from AI-powered banking and healthcare to cybersecurity, education, media distribution, IoT, and venture automation. 80+ AI agents operate across the ecosystem, powered by models like LLaMA 3.3 70B and GPT-4o.

This isn't a roadmap. This is what's live right now.

Every platform is built mobile-first, optimized for low-end devices like the Infinix Hot 10i (2GB RAM), and designed for the African market first — then the world.

Built by one founder, one AI agent (Magani), and relentless execution.
