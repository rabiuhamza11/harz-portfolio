6. Pricing Calculator Guide

The Value-Based Pricing Formula

Base Rate = (Desired Annual Income ÷ Billable Hours)

Example: $60,000 ÷ 1200 hours = $50/hour base

Multipliers: Junior (1x), Mid (1.5x), Senior (2x), Expert (3x)

Project rate = Estimated Hours × Rate × Complexity Multiplier × (1 + 20% buffer)
