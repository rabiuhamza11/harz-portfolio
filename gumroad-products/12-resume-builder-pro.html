ATS Optimization Tips

DO: Use standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
DO: Include keywords from the job description verbatim
DO: Use standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
DON'T: Use tables, columns, text boxes, or graphics
DON'T: Use creative section headings ("My Journey" instead of "Experience")
DON'T: Submit PDF if the employer specifically asks for .docx

Power Words Cheat Sheet

  • Leadership: Spearheaded, Directed, Orchestrated, Championed, Steered
  • Results: Achieved, Delivered, Exceeded, Generated, Accelerated
  • Efficiency: Streamlined, Optimized, Automated, Consolidated, Eliminated
  • Growth: Scaled, Expanded, Grew, Multiplied, Boosted
  • Innovation: Pioneered, Designed, Developed, Engineered, Launched
  • Problem-solving: Resolved, Diagnosed, Troubleshot, Remediated, Fixed
