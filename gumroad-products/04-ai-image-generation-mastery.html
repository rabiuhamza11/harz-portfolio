Module 1: Midjourney Fundamentals

Basic Prompt Structure

/imagine [subject] [style] [lighting] [composition] [aspect ratio] [quality]

Parameter Guide

  • --ar 16:9 (aspect ratio)
  • --q 2 (quality, 1-5)
  • --v 6 (version)
  • --s 750 (stylize, 0-1000)
  • --chaos 50 (variation, 0-100)
  • --no [elements to exclude]
