📐 Hook Creation Formula

The VIRAL Formula

  • V — Visual pattern interrupt (movement, text overlay, unusual angle)
  • I — Interest trigger (curiosity, shock, relatability)
  • R — Retain with value promise (what they'll learn/gain)
  • I — Information gap (don't reveal everything upfront)
  • A — Action prompt (comment, save, share trigger)
  • L — Loop potential (replayability, open-ended conclusion)
