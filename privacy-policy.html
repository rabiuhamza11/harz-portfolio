2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and deliver your digital product purchases

  • To send purchased products and download links
  • To provide customer support for 7 days after purchase
  • To send newsletter updates (only if you subscribe)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and GDPR:

  • Contract: Processing your purchase and delivering products
  • Consent: You voluntarily provide your contact information
  • Legal obligation: Tax records and financial compliance
  • Legitimate interest: Customer support and service improvement

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share only with:

  • Payment processors (Paystack, Stripe) — for transaction processing
  • Bank (UBA) — for bank transfer verification
  • Getly — for digital product delivery
  • FIRS — for tax compliance when legally required

We never share your data with marketing companies or third-party advertisers.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Data stored on secure cloud servers (Base44, GitHub, Vercel)
  • All connections encrypted via HTTPS/SSL
  • Payment data never stored on our servers — handled by PCI-compliant processors
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • 20 secrets/keys managed via secure vault
  • Daily encrypted backups to GitHub

6. Your Rights

Under NDPR and GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent — at any time

To exercise any right, email harzco.business@gmail.com or WhatsApp 08028687857.
