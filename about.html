Who We Are

Harz Digital Services is the trading name of HI Water/Block Industry, a fully registered Nigerian business operating since 2014. We started in block making and water packaging, and have evolved into a comprehensive digital services company serving entrepreneurs across Nigeria and beyond.

Today, we build and operate 24 live platforms spanning fintech, education, cybersecurity, health AI, content creation, real estate, music, film, and more — all powered by AI and designed for African businesses.
